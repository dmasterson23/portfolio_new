About

I am a web developer and digital marketer based in Atlanta, Georgia, simply put I can turn any design you have and turn it into a functioning website.
I come from a business background and working in customer service, marketing and e-commerce for years, I am very passionate about helping small businesses and startups meet their business goals. Whether that goal is increasing revenue by 20% or getting more views on your existing site. I just don’t build sites, I build solutions to your goals.

What I can do for you!

Web Design

Each site design begins the old school way with a pencil and a sketchpad. From there, it is transferred to Photoshop for your approval. Each design is simple, clean and easy to use while keeping your business goals in mind. My focus is getting the color scheme, typography and content all to flow. While at the same time, making sure the site delivers the message that you want to deliver!

Web Development

As a developer, I feel templates can limit the potential of a website, which is why I focus my development on creating unique websites for you that will work flawlessly across all devices.

Digital Marketing

Building a site is just one part of the equation, getting clients to actually come to your site is the other part. You could have the best looking site on the web, but if your site is getting no traffic what good is it. I’ll use the latest SEO techniques and strategies to optimize your site, get more visitors to your site and help your company drive more revenue.
